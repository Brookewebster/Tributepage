Spencer Ryan Webster

1987-2011

Spencers Life

Spencer Ryan Webster of Tempe, AZ passed away on Saturday, November 12, 2011 at the tender age of 24. Spencer was born June 4, 1987, in Provo, Utah, to Mark Webster and Desiree Webster Anderson. Spencer graduated from Highland High School in Gilbert in 2005 and was pursuing a degree in Journalism at ASU. Spencer was loved by everyone who knew him and would light up a room with his personality and smile. He loved to explore and try new things, he loved music and played the guitar and piano. Spencer was a gifted writer and had a passion and knowledge of current events. Spencer was able to see the world through travel and spent parts of two summers in Europe with some of his great friends. See the rest of his obituary here.
